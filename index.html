Our Mission

First and foremost, the goal of engiBEERing is to help make great beer! Founded by two craft beer aficionados with degrees in Engineering Physics, engiBEERing seeks to apply novel techniques in electromechanical system design, data analytics and internet of things in search of that perfect brew. Based in the craft beer capital of the world (Vancouver, British Columbia) engiBEERing offers a diverse range of expertise for the needs of brewing operations large and small. Our solutions suit both the immediate needs of brewing operations or can offer novel research avenues for long term growth. At the end of the day, our team wants to work on interesting and challenging projects that focus on the important things in life, namely beer!
