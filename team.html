Chief Technology Officer Toren Dofher

Toren Dofher is a Engineering Physics Graduate with a Minor in Honours Mathematics. Through his years in engineering he has worked extensively in data science and electrical systems, and also conducted research in viscous fluids. As a self respecting west coaster he enjoys a nice hoppy IPA, but also takes a keen interest in stouts, sours and saisons. His current batch of home brew, co-brewed with his housemates as The Estate Brewing Co., is an amarillo showcasing DIPA.

Chief Science Officer Owen Bulka

He loves stouts and porters in the winter, lagers on the beach, and triple IPAs wherever they can be found.
